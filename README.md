# EcoSprint

7 days. 60kg CO2. GO.

A little game I built where you try to keep your carbon footprint under budget for a week. Pick your commute, your lunch, how you chill at home — every choice adds up. Go over budget and you lose. Stay under and you win.

Built this for an MLH hackathon challenge. Wanted to make climate numbers less boring and more like... well, a game.

## How to Play

You get 7 days. Each day gives you a choice with a real carbon cost. Pick wisely — your budget is only 60kg CO2 for the whole week.

- Good choices = low carbon (bike, veggie food, thrift shopping)
- Bad choices = high carbon (drive alone, beef, fast fashion)

After day 7, you get your score + personalized tips based on what you actually chose.

## Why I Made This

The average weekly carbon footprint is around 120kg CO2. Most people don't know that. Or what adds up. This game shows you — without being preachy. You mess up, you restart, you learn.

Made this for MLH Global Hack Week. Copilot did the heavy lifting, I just had the ideas.

## Tips You'll See

- Beef = 25kg per meal. Yeah, that's a lot.
- One flight = 85kg. That's your whole budget gone.
- Thrift shopping = 1kg. Fast fashion = 22kg. You do the math.

## That's It

Go play. Try to win. It's harder than it looks.

If you beat the budget, you're doing better than most. If you don't, well... now you know what to change.
